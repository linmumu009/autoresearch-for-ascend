# v0.3.6 - 4e-5 12-Step Verification And 5e-5 Probe

Date: 2026-07-03

## What Changed

- Added `framework_adapters/mindspeed_llm/candidates/lr_4em5_12step.env`.
- Added `framework_adapters/mindspeed_llm/candidates/lr_5em5_6step.env`.
- Updated English and Chinese README files, framework evaluation notes,
  changelog, and MindSpeed-LLM adapter notes with the new results.

## Why

The v0.3.5 run showed that `LR=4.0e-5` was better than `3.0e-5` under the
6-step boundary search, while `3.0e-5` was the strongest 12-step result. This
version verifies `4.0e-5` under the same 12-step budget and probes `5.0e-5` on
the shorter boundary run.

## Results

| Candidate | LR | Steps | Raw HF Val Loss | Raw HF Val PPL | MindSpeed Valid Loss | Last Train Loss |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| `lr_4em5_12step.env` | `4.0e-5` | 12 | 12.668455 | 317570.340 | 0.324619 | 0.492905 |
| `lr_5em5_6step.env` | `5.0e-5` | 6 | 12.643408 | 309714.910 | 0.326887 | 0.528050 |

## Decision

Keep `lr_5em5_6step.env` as the best observed candidate overall.

The 6-step over-shoot boundary is still above `5.0e-5`. Under the 12-step
budget, `4.0e-5` beats `3.0e-5`, so the longer-budget optimum has also moved
upward.

## Next Step

Verify `5.0e-5` under the same 12-step budget, or run a cautious `6.0e-5`
6-step candidate to keep mapping the short-budget boundary.
