# v0.3.7 - 5e-5 12-Step Verification And 6e-5 Probe

Date: 2026-07-03

## What Changed

- Added `framework_adapters/mindspeed_llm/candidates/lr_5em5_12step.env`.
- Added `framework_adapters/mindspeed_llm/candidates/lr_6em5_6step.env`.
- Updated English and Chinese README files, framework evaluation notes,
  changelog, and MindSpeed-LLM adapter notes with the new results.

## Why

The v0.3.6 run showed that `LR=5.0e-5` was the best 6-step candidate, while
`LR=4.0e-5` was the strongest 12-step result. This version verifies `5.0e-5`
under the 12-step budget and probes `6.0e-5` on the shorter boundary run.

## Results

| Candidate | LR | Steps | Raw HF Val Loss | Raw HF Val PPL | MindSpeed Valid Loss | Last Train Loss |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| `lr_5em5_12step.env` | `5.0e-5` | 12 | 12.686586 | 323380.772 | 0.349943 | 0.516511 |
| `lr_6em5_6step.env` | `6.0e-5` | 6 | 12.540532 | 279436.825 | 0.347458 | 0.555464 |

## Decision

Keep `lr_6em5_6step.env` as the best observed candidate overall.

The 12-step budget now shows an over-shoot sign: `5.0e-5` is worse than
`4.0e-5`. The 6-step boundary still has not over-shot at `6.0e-5`.

## Next Step

Refine the 12-step boundary around `4.5e-5`, or continue the short-budget search
with `7.0e-5` to find the 6-step over-shoot point.
